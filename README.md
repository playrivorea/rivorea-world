# 🌊 Rivorea

> **Luxury shared-world MMO on Solana**  
> Start broke on a Mediterranean island. Build, trade, and rise to the top.

[Website](https://rivorea.com/) • [X (Twitter)](https://x.com/playrivorea)

---

## 🏝️ Overview

**Rivorea** is a luxury open-world MMO set in a living Mediterranean-inspired archipelago, built on the **Solana blockchain**.

Every player begins with nothing—washed ashore on a sun-drenched island with limited resources. Through exploration, crafting, trading, and strategic social gameplay, players rise from survival to wealth, influence, and dominance in a fully shared economy.

This is not just a game—it’s a **player-driven society**.

---

## 🌍 Core Fantasy

- Start broke on a forgotten island
- Gather, craft, and survive
- Build property, businesses, and trade routes
- Form alliances and economies
- Become rich, powerful, or legendary

Every decision shapes your path in Rivorea’s living world.

---

## ⚙️ Core Gameplay Loops

### ⛏️ Gather & Survive
- Collect natural resources from islands
- Fish, mine, farm, and scavenge
- Upgrade survival tools and skills

### 🏗️ Build & Expand
- Construct homes, shops, and estates
- Develop island infrastructure
- Unlock luxury upgrades and territories

### 💱 Trade & Economy
- Player-driven marketplace
- Supply chain-based economy
- Dynamic pricing based on demand

### 🤝 Social & Politics
- Form guilds and factions
- Compete for island control
- Influence regional economies

---

## 🧠 Economy Design

Rivorea is powered by a **decentralized, player-owned economy**:

- On-chain asset ownership (Solana)
- Tradeable in-game resources
- Real value tied to player activity
- Scarcity driven by world dynamics

---

## 🔗 Blockchain Integration

Built on **Solana**, Rivorea leverages:

- Fast, low-cost transactions
- NFT-based assets (land, items, structures)
- Wallet-based identity system
- Open marketplace interoperability
